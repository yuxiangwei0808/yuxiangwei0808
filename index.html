Research

Brain modeling, generative AI, and reliable learning systems.

fMRI Foundation Models

Building multimodal models that align fMRI tokens with language and support reasoning over complex brain activation patterns.

Brain Visual Decoding

Designing interpretable mixture-of-experts and diffusion-based systems for image generation from brain activity across subjects.

Biomedical Machine Learning

Modeling dynamic brain connectivity and early Alzheimer's risk with state-space models, contrastive learning, and knowledge distillation.

Agentic Memory

Studying long-term dialog memory and AI clone benchmarks through modular representation, organization, retrieval, and maintenance.

Forecasting and Uplift

Developing graph-aware probabilistic forecasting and causal uplift models for large-scale applied machine learning systems.
